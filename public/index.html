Common Questions

How can you get in touch?

Simply send us an email at: contact@djikefactor.com With the details of your query and your country of residence. One of our representatives will be in touch.

What are the administration fees?

Invoices between D100,000 and D1,500,000 fees are D10,000. Invoices between D1,500,001 and D3,000,000 fees are D20,000. Invoices above D3,000,001 fees are D30,000.

How long will it take to fulfill my request?

Once you have qualified for Djikéfactor’s financing solution, we will pay your invoice in 72 hours or less. Your goods will then be delivered to your warehouse (please allow X weeks for delivery depending on the country the goods have been shipped from).

How much does it cost?

We take 3% commission on the currency you give us. We also take 18% to cover the VAT costs associated with your transfer.
